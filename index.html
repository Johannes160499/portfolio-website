Nicholas Bhero: Empowering Success and Driving Change

Nicholas Bhero is more than just a name on a business card; he's a beacon of inspiration who ignites the potential within individuals and communities. So, if you're looking for more than just business expertise, if you seek inspiration and guidance on your journey towards personal and professional fulfillment, then look no further than Nicholas Bhero. He is a testament to the power of vision, dedication, and a heart that extends beyond self.

Val OkaChief

Val Okafor is a Leadership and Productivity Strategist. He's a Transformational Speaker and Social Innovator who possesses an unwavering commitment to catalysing exponential growth for individuals and organizations. His practical knowledge transcends mere information, providing invaluable wisdom that generates tangible results.

At The Val Okafor Company, he serves as the Chief Executive Officer and training consultant, delivering value-based leadership development and personal effectiveness training programs.

His contributions extend beyond his professional endeavors. As a Nigerian youth SDGs Champion, African Change Makers fellow, Commonwealth Open Source Leader, fellow of the YALI Regional Leadership Conference West Africa, and proud ambassador of the Fela Durotoye Leadership Network, he embodies a deep commitment to making a lasting impact on society.

Val's unyielding passion for Nation Building and Human Capital Development, particularly in developing nations worldwide, is palpable. He firmly believes that by allowing one's own light to shine, the ripple effect empowers others to do the same.

He is not just a leader; he is a catalyst for transformation, an advocate for progress, and an inspiration to all those who strive to make a meaningful difference in the world.

Vanessa Ukamaka Richard-Bassey

Vanessa Ukamaka Richard-Bassey, a seasoned broadcast journalist with over 7 years of impactful storytelling, is a dedicated advocate for "Solutions Journalism" and health communication. From her humble beginnings as a volunteer reporter at UNIZIK 94.1FM to leading a 15-person team, Vanessa's journey is marked by a commitment to amplifying voices and shedding light on crucial issues.

During her National Youth Service Corps, Vanessa organized a groundbreaking radio talk show addressing menstruation challenges, sparking community involvement and assistance for girls in need. Presently, as the News Anchor and talk show host at Sparkling 92.3FM, she delves into in-depth reporting on politics, governance, and gender issues, earning her a nomination for the REPORT HER AWARDS in 2023.

Vanessa's dedication extends to fact-checking, collaborating with Africa Check to ensure accuracy in reporting. Amidst the COVID-19 pandemic, she initiated a health program connecting medical experts with the grassroots, showcasing her adaptability and commitment to public welfare.

Recognized in 2023 as part of the Media-EIS fellowship program, Vanessa is a certified solutions journalist, illuminating stories of progress in Cross River State communities. Beyond radio, she hosts the Impact Africa Podcast and Webinar series, engaging with industry leaders to educate and empower young minds.

Vanessa's forward-thinking approach and advocacy for better policies showcase her commitment to societal improvement. Married to real estate consultant Godwin Bassey Bassey, she continues to embrace new opportunities, eager to learn and contribute more to the betterment of society.

Prechard Mhako


Summary

Prechard Mhako is an impact entrepreneur & respected innovation, strategy, sustainability and enterprise development advisor to entrepreneurs, startups, large corporations, business associations, and educational institutions. He is a 2019 Mandela Washington Fellow as well as a 2023 Obama Leader.

Prechard is a well-respected expert on innovation and entrepreneurship; in fact, numerous organizations, such as ZIMTRADE, the Confederation of Zimbabwean Industries, and the Zimbabwe National Chamber of Commerce, frequently ask him to speak on the topic.

At Abiyedu Kin Consulting, a strategy consulting firm that supports innovative entrepreneurship, Prechard Mhako is co-founder and managing partner. He is also the co-founder and chief commercial officer at Energyneering Zimbabwe, a company providing underserved communities with affordable renewable electricity and safe drinking water. Prechard has partnered with international development partners to deliver innovative solutions, such as a water container that purifies contaminated water using solar energy.

He learnt about entrepreneurship at a young age from his father, who owned a rural business that provided many impactful jobs in the community, this is where be derives his strong community values. Prechard’s personal goal is to help create 10,000 sustainable jobs.

Initially, Prechard worked for the Construction Industry Pension Fund, where he assessed, developed, and oversaw a large property investment portfolio serving a wide range of corporate clients. After that, he joined Sandton, South Africa-based Astro Mobile Africa, a mobile tech upmarket company, where he worked as the group CEO's executive assistant until switching to full time entrepreneurship. His entrepreneurial endeavors have brought him into contact with industries like manufacturing, renewable energy, and consultancy.

In 2013 he served as business development consultant to one of Zimbabwe’s biggest football Clubs, CAPS United FC. In 2016 was involved in the maiden Aliko Dangote visit to Zimbabwe. He has advised over 100 Startups. SMEs and Large Business Corporations. Prechard’s most recent success involves leading the Petrotrade Board through an ESG strategy program.
